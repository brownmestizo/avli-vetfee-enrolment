avli-vetfee-enrolment
=====================

VET FEE Enrolment Form for AVLI

This form is a re-developed version of AVLI's Enrolment Form for its VET FEE students. This form utilised Stefan Gabos' Zebra Form library after an exhaustive research of the best PHP-jQuery form solutions. 

This form submits to the company's CRM and also submits information to JobReady.
